# Accommodation Research Skill

## Platform Priority

- Airbnb preferred (per family profile `accommodation_style: airbnb_preferred`)
- Booking.com as secondary comparison — useful for villas and aparthotels
- Hotels only if the destination doesn't suit Airbnb (e.g., short city break)

## Minimum Requirements (from family profile)

- 4+ bedrooms (family of 6: 2 parents + 4 children)
- Full kitchen (not kitchenette)
- Private garden or terrace
- Houses/villas only — filter out apartments, shared spaces, and hotel rooms

## Search Strategy

- On Airbnb: set filters for 6 guests, 4+ bedrooms, "Entire home", target dates, target area
- Sort by rating first (4.5+ stars preferred), then price
- On Booking.com: search "villas" or "holiday homes" with same filters
- If the user specified an area, search that area first; if no results, broaden to the wider region

## Evaluation Criteria

- Distance to beach (if beach trip) or city centre (if city trip)
- Superhost/top-rated host status (reliability indicator)
- Cancellation policy (prefer flexible or moderate)
- Reviews mentioning families or children specifically
- Parking availability (need space for a 7-seat rental vehicle)
- Pool or proximity to family-friendly beach

## Car Rental Note

- Car rental is part of the accommodation context: always confirm a 7-seat vehicle (minivan or large SUV) is available at the destination airport for the trip dates
- Estimate car rental cost as part of the accommodation summary

## Presentation

- Present each option with: name, property type, nightly rate, total, bedrooms/bathrooms, key amenities, distance to beach/centre, rating, and booking URL
- Include a brief "why this one" note for each option
