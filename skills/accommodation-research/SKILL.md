# Accommodation Research Skill

> **Stub — implemented in Milestone 5**

This skill will encode:

- Airbnb preferred over hotels (unless hotel makes more sense for the destination)
- Minimum requirements: 4 bedrooms, full kitchen, private garden or terrace
- Search strategy for Airbnb and Booking.com with family filters
- Sort by rating first, then price
- Filter out apartments and shared spaces — want houses/villas
- Car rental requirements: 7-seat minimum (family of 6 + luggage)
- How to present accommodation options with key amenities and booking links
