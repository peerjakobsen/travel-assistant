# Itinerary Builder Skill

> **Stub — implemented in Milestone 7**

This skill will encode:

- Maximum 2 structured activities per day with kids
- Buffer/beach/free day every 3-4 days
- Check opening days/hours for attractions (many close Monday/Tuesday)
- Flag activities needing pre-booking (popular restaurants, theme parks, skip-the-line)
- Group activities geographically to minimise driving
- Include 1-2 rainy day backup options
- Age-appropriate activity filtering based on children's ages at travel time
